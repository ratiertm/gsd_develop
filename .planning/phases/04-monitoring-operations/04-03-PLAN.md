     - QListWidget showing stock codes from settings._config["watchlist"].

     - `pg.GraphicsLayoutWidget` with:

     - Checkboxes: SMA, EMA, Bollinger, VWAP (price overlays), RSI, MACD, OBV (sub-charts).

     - Per user decision: "checkbox toggle ON/OFF with immediate chart update."

     - `_candle_buffers: dict[str, list[tuple]]` -- per-stock candle history, max 120 entries (sliding window).

     - `_trade_markers: dict[str, list[tuple]]` -- per-stock trade markers (index, price, side).

   - **Key methods**:
